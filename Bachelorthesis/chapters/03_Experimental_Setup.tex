\section{Sample configuration}
For the FMR measurements performed during the course of this thesis, samples with a symmetric stack (BRAUCH ICH WIRKLICH EIN GANZES UNTERKAPITEL FÜR DIE STAKCS?) of Platinum, Niobium, and Permalloy were used, as shown in figure \ref{Stack_Foto}. The measurements were only done on samples with a Permalloy layer thickness of 45nm and 30nm. This was due to the fact that the cryostat used could not hold the temperature below the critical temperatures of the thinner samples in a stable manner. The samples were placed ???film up??? on a continuous waveguide (CPW), which was then placed into the tip of an insert that got submerged into liquid helium in a cryostat. An illustration of this setup is shown at figure \ref{Insert setup}.
